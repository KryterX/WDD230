Temple Activities

While attending the temple there are a few different activites that you can choose to participate in during your stay. These activities include: Baptisms for the Dead, Initiatories, Endownments, and Sealings. All these activities can be completed by adults and for the Baptisms for the Dead you can have anyone ages 12 and up.

San Diego Temple Summary

The San Diego California Temple is a temple of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints (LDS Church) just north of San Diego. The intent to construct the temple was announced by church president Hinkley, during the church's semi-annual general conference. The temple is 72,000 square feet and is located on a 7.4-acre site.
